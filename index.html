About Health Optimizer

This study will focus on innovative techniques for developing a mobile system that tracks and provides timely analysis of health information with modern mobile sensors and devices such as smartphones, smartwatches, fitness trackers.

Through use of survey questions we will be exploring potential connections between productivity optimization and health data.
